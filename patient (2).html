What do these levels mean? ⌄
Red — High Risk
NEWS2 Score 7 or higher
Urgent or emergency response required. Critical care team involvement with airway management capability.
Orange — Medium Risk
NEWS2 Score 5 to 6
Key threshold for urgent response. Clinician with competence in acute care assessment required.
Yellow — Low to Medium
Score of 3 in any single parameter (Red Flag)
Urgent ward-based response. Clinician assessment with consideration of escalation to acute care team.
Green — Low Risk
NEWS2 Score 0 to 4 with no Red Flag
Ward-based monitoring response. Routine care with continued monitoring of vital signs.
